# Blind Solver Prompt

You are solving a mathematical task from its statement only.

Do not make novelty claims. Do not infer publication status. Treat the statement as possibly true, false, misstated, or only partially true.

Return:

1. Exact statement considered
2. Edge cases and counterexample search
3. Main proof or disproof attempt
4. Lemmas used
5. Gaps or uncertain steps
6. Suggested formalization target
